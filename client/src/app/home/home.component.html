Iterate is all about becoming a better developer and better person. We think a big part of being a better developer and person is contributing back to the development community.

If contributing back to the developer community is something you also believe in, please donate either time (or money) to open source. We've teamed up with OpenCollective (a non-profit which distributes donations to a number of large open source projects) to collect funds.

By pledging your money below you're directly helping other creators like yourself and supporting open source development for the entire community.

If you can't or don't want to donate money: you can also pledge to donate your time. Donating time or contribute to an open source project can be a great experience for you and make a lasting positive impact on software that developers use every day.
